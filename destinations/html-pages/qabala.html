About This Place

Gabala, located in the foothills of the Greater Caucasus Mountains, is Azerbaijan's premier mountain resort destination. This ancient city, once the capital of Caucasian Albania, has transformed into a modern tourism hub while preserving its rich historical heritage. The region offers breathtaking mountain landscapes, crystal-clear lakes, and a perfect blend of adventure activities and cultural experiences.

The crown jewel of Gabala is the Tufandag Mountain Resort, featuring a state-of-the-art cable car system that provides spectacular panoramic views of the Caucasus Mountains. Visitors can enjoy year-round activities including skiing in winter, hiking in summer, and exploring the pristine Nohur Lake. The city also boasts ancient archaeological sites, traditional villages, and the famous Gabaland amusement park. With its fresh mountain air, scenic beauty, and excellent tourist infrastructure, Gabala has become a favorite destination for both adventure seekers and those looking for peaceful mountain retreats.
