Nohur Lake

About This Place

Nohur Lake, nestled in the heart of the Gabala region, is one of Azerbaijan's most beautiful natural attractions. This pristine mountain lake sits at an elevation of 700 meters above sea level, surrounded by the lush forests and dramatic peaks of the Greater Caucasus Mountains. The lake's crystal-clear waters perfectly reflect the surrounding landscape, creating breathtaking mirror-like views that change with the seasons and time of day.

Originally created as a reservoir, Nohur Lake has evolved into a popular destination for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. The area offers excellent opportunities for fishing, boating, and peaceful lakeside walks along well-maintained trails. Visitors can enjoy boat rides across the calm waters, picnic in designated areas, or simply relax while taking in the spectacular mountain scenery. The lake is also a perfect spot for photography, especially during sunrise and sunset when the water becomes a mirror reflecting the golden light on the surrounding peaks. The lake serves as an excellent base for exploring the wider Gabala region, including the nearby Tufandag Mountain Resort and traditional mountain villages, making it a must-visit destination for anyone exploring Azerbaijan's natural beauty.
