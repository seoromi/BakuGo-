Mud Volcanoes

Witness Earth's unique geological wonders ‚Äî where bubbling mud craters create an otherworldly landscape unlike anywhere else on the planet.

About This Place

The Mud Volcanoes of Azerbaijan offer one of the most unique natural spectacles in the world. Home to nearly half of the planet's mud volcanoes, Azerbaijan's landscape is dotted with these bubbling, otherworldly formations ‚Äî especially around the Gobustan region. These geological wonders are formed by underground gas emissions pushing mud and water to the surface, creating cone-shaped structures that continuously bubble and sometimes erupt with dramatic force.

These volcanoes, which release gas, mud, and sometimes flames, create a lunar-like terrain that attracts both scientists and curious travelers. Despite their name, mud volcanoes are generally harmless and can range from small mounds to towering cones reaching several meters in height. Their cool, mineral-rich mud is also believed to have therapeutic properties, and many visitors enjoy the unique experience of walking through this Martian-like landscape. The area around Gobustan is home to the most accessible mud volcanoes, where visitors can safely observe these natural phenomena and experience the surreal feeling of standing on an active geological site.
