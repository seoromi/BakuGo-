About This Place

Shahdag, standing at 4,243 meters, is Azerbaijan's highest mountain and the crown jewel of the Greater Caucasus range. Located in the Shahdag National Park, this majestic peak offers some of the most spectacular mountain scenery in the Caucasus region. The area has been developed into a world-class mountain resort destination, featuring modern ski facilities, luxury accommodations, and year-round outdoor activities that rival the best alpine destinations in Europe.

The Shahdag Mountain Resort provides visitors with state-of-the-art cable car systems, professional ski slopes, and extensive hiking trails that showcase the region's incredible biodiversity and pristine wilderness. In winter, the resort transforms into a premier skiing destination with reliable snow coverage and modern facilities. During summer months, the area offers exceptional hiking, mountain biking, and nature photography opportunities with access to alpine meadows, glacial lakes, and panoramic viewpoints. The resort combines luxury amenities with authentic Caucasian mountain culture, offering visitors a perfect blend of adventure and comfort in one of Azerbaijan's most spectacular natural settings.
