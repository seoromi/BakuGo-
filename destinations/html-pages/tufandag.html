About This Place

Tufandag Mountain Resort, located near the picturesque city of Gabala, is one of Azerbaijan's top destinations for winter sports and mountain tourism. Surrounded by the Greater Caucasus Mountains, the resort offers stunning natural scenery and modern facilities for skiing, snowboarding, hiking, and cable car rides.

In winter, Tufandag transforms into a snowy wonderland ideal for both beginners and seasoned skiers. In summer, the area becomes a lush alpine escape perfect for hiking, mountain biking, and enjoying panoramic views from the cable car. With luxury hotels, restaurants, and spa facilities on site, it's a year-round destination that blends adventure with comfort.
