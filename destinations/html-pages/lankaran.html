Lankaran

Discover Azerbaijan's subtropical paradise ‚Äî where tea plantations, thermal springs, and Caspian shores create a unique southern experience.

About This Place

Lankaran, located in southern Azerbaijan near the Iranian border, represents the country's subtropical paradise. This unique region boasts a dramatically different climate and landscape from the rest of Azerbaijan, featuring lush tea plantations, thermal springs, and pristine Caspian Sea beaches. The area is renowned for its tea production, being one of the northernmost tea-growing regions in the world, and its rich biodiversity including rare birds and plants.

The region combines natural wonders with cultural heritage, featuring the ancient Hirkan National Park ‚Äî a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve that preserves remnants of prehistoric forests. Visitors can explore traditional tea factories, relax in natural thermal springs, and experience the unique Talysh culture with its distinct language and customs. The nearby Astara border town offers additional cultural experiences and shopping opportunities. Lankaran's coastal location provides beautiful sandy beaches along the Caspian Sea, while inland areas feature hot springs, rice fields, and citrus groves, creating a diverse landscape perfect for both relaxation and adventure.
