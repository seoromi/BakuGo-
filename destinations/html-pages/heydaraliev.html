Heydar Aliyev Center

Step into the fluid beauty of Baku's skyline at the Heydar Aliyev Center ‚Äî where architecture meets imagination.

About This Place

The Heydar Aliyev Center is one of Azerbaijan's most iconic symbols of modern architecture and innovation. Designed by world-renowned architect Zaha Hadid and completed in 2012, this futuristic structure stands out in Baku's skyline with its flowing curves and seamless, organic form ‚Äî a striking departure from traditional architectural conventions. The building's revolutionary design features no sharp angles, creating a continuous surface that appears to flow from the ground into the sky.

More than just a building, the Center is a cultural hub that houses exhibitions, performances, and events celebrating Azerbaijani heritage and global creativity. Its fluid design reflects a spirit of openness and progress, while the pristine white surface and sculptural silhouette make it a masterpiece of contemporary design admired around the world. Inside, visitors can explore interactive exhibitions showcasing Azerbaijan's history, culture, and achievements, along with rotating displays of international art and design. The center also features a world-class auditorium that hosts concerts, conferences, and cultural performances.
