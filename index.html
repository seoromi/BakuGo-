Discover the Magic of Baku

Baku, the capital of Azerbaijan, is a fascinating city where ancient history meets modern innovation. Known as the "City of Winds," Baku offers visitors an extraordinary blend of medieval architecture, futuristic skyscrapers, and rich cultural heritage.

From the UNESCO World Heritage-listed Old City (Icherisheher) with its ancient Maiden Tower and Palace of the Shirvanshahs, to the modern Flame Towers that light up the night sky, Baku presents a unique contrast of old and new.

Experience the warmth of Azerbaijani hospitality, savor delicious local cuisine, and explore the city's vibrant arts scene. Whether you're wandering through narrow cobblestone streets or admiring contemporary architecture, Baku promises unforgettable memories.
