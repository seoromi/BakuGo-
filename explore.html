Currency & Costs

The currency is Azerbaijani Manat (AZN). Azerbaijan offers excellent value for money with affordable food, transport, and accommodation options.

Food Culture

Cultural Etiquette

Welcome to Azerbaijan

Azerbaijan, known as the "Land of Fire," is a captivating country where ancient traditions meet modern innovation. Located at the crossroads of Europe and Asia, this remarkable nation offers visitors an extraordinary blend of historical treasures and contemporary marvels.

From the eternal flames of Yanardag to the futuristic architecture of the Heydar Aliyev Center, from ancient petroglyphs in Gobustan to the medieval charm of Sheki, Azerbaijan provides unforgettable experiences for every traveler.

Discover a land where fire temples tell stories of ancient civilizations, where mud volcanoes create otherworldly landscapes, and where warm hospitality makes every visitor feel at home.
